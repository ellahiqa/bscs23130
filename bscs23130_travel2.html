Places I Want to Travel

Traveling is one of my greatest passions, and I have a long list of places I want to visit in the future. Here are some of the destinations on my travel bucket list:

  • Paris, France - To see the Eiffel Tower and experience the romantic atmosphere of the city.
  • Tokyo, Japan - To immerse myself in the vibrant culture, try authentic sushi, and explore the bustling city.
  • Santorini, Greece - To enjoy the stunning sunsets, beautiful beaches, and white-washed buildings.
  • Machu Picchu, Peru - To hike the ancient Inca Trail and witness the breathtaking ruins.
